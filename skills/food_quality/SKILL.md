# Food Quality Grading

This skill grades food quality based on visual and provided nutritional or text descriptions.
Assign one of the following grades based on your analysis:
- **Grade A:** Premium fresh food.
- **Grade B:** Acceptable quality.
- **Grade C:** Poor quality.

Analyze the following properties:
- Color
- Texture
- Damage
- Freshness
