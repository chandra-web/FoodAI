# Food Vision Analysis & Quality Grading

This skill provides guidelines for analyzing food images.

## Vision Analysis
When you receive an image of food, analyze it to:
- Identify the food item
- Analyze its freshness
- Detect any defects (bruises, mold, wilting, etc.)
- Estimate overall quality

## Quality Grading System
You MUST assign one of the following grades based on your analysis:
- **Grade A:** Premium fresh food. Vibrant color, firm texture, no defects, peak freshness.
- **Grade B:** Acceptable quality. Minor blemishes, slightly dull color, still perfectly edible.
- **Grade C:** Poor quality. Significant defects, wilting, discoloration, approaching or past spoilage.

When grading, provide an assessment of:
- Color
- Texture
- Damage
- Freshness
